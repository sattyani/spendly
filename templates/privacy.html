Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

1. Overview

Spendly ("we", "us", or "the Service") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

By using Spendly, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your email address and hashed password when you register
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually
  • Usage data — basic technical information such as browser type and pages visited, used solely to maintain and improve the Service

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account data.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve the reliability of the Service

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secured servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We apply industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your expense records and personal information will be permanently removed from our systems.

6. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — to load typefaces used in the interface. Google may collect basic request metadata per their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms that track individuals across sites, or any service that receives your expense data.

7. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie is essential for the Service to function and contains no personally identifiable information beyond a session identifier. It is deleted when you log out or clear your browser cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our website. We will respond as promptly as possible.
